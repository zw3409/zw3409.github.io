I am Zichu Wang, a PhD student at the [MIT Center for Computational Science and Engineering](https://cse.mit.edu/) in the [Massachusetts Institute of Technology](https://www.mit.edu/), advised by [Youssef Marzouk](https://aeroastro.mit.edu/people/youssef-m-marzouk/). My research interests lie broadly in applied mathematics, with a focus on sampling, measure transport, and generative modeling. Previously, I was an undergraduate student in Mathematics at the [Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences](https://cims.nyu.edu/), [New York University](https://www.nyu.edu/).
